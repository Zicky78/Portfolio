Projects

Simon Game



Description: A clone of the toy / memory game Simon. The computer generates a color on its turn, plays it, and stores it. The player must then play the correct color, or sequence of colors. The chain of colors gets progressively longer, and if the player presses an incorrect color, game over.

Snake Game



Description: A clone of the classic game Snake. The goal is to navigate around the field to eat the apples that generate. Doing so grows the snake bigger, and makes the score go up. This comes with a risk though, because if you touch the wall, or accidently nom on yourself, its game over.
